Help & FAQ

How do I book the Showroom or Conference Room?

Click Book Now, pick a date on the calendar, choose an available time slot, then fill in the Agenda and other details. Some bookings are auto-confirmed; others may need admin approval first.

How do I cancel or reschedule a booking?

Go to My Bookings, find the booking, and use the Cancel or Reschedule button. This is only available before the booking's start time.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

On the login screen, click Forgot password? (available for both Employee and Admin logins). You'll receive a 6-digit OTP by email — enter it to set a new password.

How do I change my password?

Click your name/avatar in the top-right to open My Profile, then use the Change Password section near the bottom.

Why is my booking still "Pending"?

Some bookings require admin review before they're confirmed. You'll get an email once it's approved or if any changes are needed.

Can I book a room from the Employee Portal instead?

Yes — click Book a Room in the Employee Portal and you'll be brought here already signed in, no need to log in again.

Still need help?

Contact your system administrator or Isaac / Joymee / Ma'am Ana.
